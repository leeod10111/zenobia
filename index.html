About Zenobia

Inspired by the legendary Queen Zenobia of Palmyra, Zenobia celebrates the richness of Middle Eastern cuisine with a contemporary, refined twist. Our chefs craft each dish with time-honoured techniques, fragrant spices, and seasonal ingredients.

From velvety hummus and smoky baba ghanoush to slow-braised lamb and charcoal-grilled seafood, every plate is designed for sharing and savouring together.

Step into a space of dark tones, golden details, and warm hospitality—an intimate setting for late evenings, celebrations, and everything in between.
