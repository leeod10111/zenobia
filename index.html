About Zenobia

Inspired by the legendary Queen Zenobia of Palmyra, Zenobia is a tribute to the elegance and depth of Middle Eastern gastronomy, reimagined with a refined contemporary touch.

Our kitchen honours time-hallowed traditions, where fragrant spices, slow craftsmanship, and the finest seasonal ingredients come together in quiet harmony. From silken hummus and delicately smoked moutabal to expertly charcoal-grilled meats, each dish is composed to be an invitation to savour, connect, and indulge.

Golden details, soft light, and the gentle perfume of charcoal create an atmosphere of understated luxury. Zenobia is a place for lingering evenings, intimate celebrations, and moments that unfold slowly — just as exceptional dining should.
